Privacy Policy

We respect each individual’s right to personal privacy. We will collect and use information through our web site only in the ways disclosed in this statement. This statement applies solely to information collected at the delekus.com web site.

Information Collection

We do not employ the use of “cookies” on our web site. A “cookie” is a small text file that many web servers place on a user’s computer hard drive to be a unique identifier. Cookies enable many companies to track usage patterns and deliver customized content to users.

 

Information Usage

Since we do not employ any means of tracking web site visitor information, we do not maintain any information about website visitors or use that information in any manner

At our discretion, we may offer links to other web sites that may provide additional information to you. When you click on links to other sites, a new window will appear to indicate that you have left the delekus.com web site. We strongly encourage you to read their privacy policies. Their standards may differ from ours.

We do not rent or sell customer lists to other companies, and no personal information is collected directly by this web site This web site contains links that allow you to send information to us by e-mail.  We use the information you send to us by e-mail to respond to your inquiry or request, and otherwise in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use for this web site. We are not responsible for lost, misdirected, intercepted or damaged e-mail communications.

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.  We are not responsible for any harm that you or any person may suffer as a result of a breach of confidentiality in respect to your use of this site or any information you transmitted to this site.

If for any reason our policy on information collection or uses changes, we will alert our web site visitors by placing such notice on our web site in our Privacy Policy statement.
