We use third parties to provide certain Services. Some of our Services may contain links to third parties’ websites. This Privacy Policy does not address, and we do not control and are not responsible for the content, security or privacy practices of any third parties, including any third party operating any site or service to which the Services link.  The inclusion of a link on the Services does not imply endorsement of the linked site or service by us or by our affiliates. 


 


In addition, we are not responsible for the information collection, use, disclosure or security policies or practices of other organizations, such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Pinterest (collectively “Social Media Pages”) Apple, Google, Microsoft, RIM or any other app developer, app provider, social media platform provider, operating system provider, wireless service provider or device manufacturer, including any information you disclose to other organizations through or in connection with the Apps or our Social Media Pages.  


 


The Services are operated in the United States and are not intended for use by residents of any country outside of the US. The Services and our collection, use and sharing of your information is governed solely by US law.


 


3.             What information is collected by Dollar General? 


 


We collect  information when you interact with us, including, but not limited to, your first and last name, home or mailing address, e-mail address and telephone number.  In some circumstances, we (or our service providers) may also collect: date of birth or age; driver’s license number; purchase/return/exchange information; list information; information concerning your mobile device (including, without limitation, browser and device information); information concerning how you use our Websites and Apps; certain debit/credit card information; geo-location and in-store location information; information collected through cookies, pixel tags and other technologies; temporary or persistent unique device identifiers; and/or demographic information.


 


If you submit any information relating to other people to us or to our service providers in connection with the Services, you represent that you have the authority to do so and to permit us to use the information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

You can stop all collection of such information by the Apps by uninstalling the Application. You may use the standard uninstall processes as may be available as part of the mobile device’s operating system or via the mobile application marketplace or network.

  • By aggregating information: Aggregated information does not personally identify you or any other user of the Services (for example, we may aggregate personal information to calculate the percentage of our users who have a particular telephone area code).
