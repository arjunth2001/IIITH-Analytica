Information We Collect

When you use the Services, we collect certain information, either actively or passively. Passively-collected information typically includes your IP address, browser type and browser configuration. Our cookies help us recognize you as a repeat visitor and help you browse our websites and use other aspects of the Services more efficiently. You can configure your computer or mobile device to reject or delete cookies. We also actively collect information you give us, depending on how you are using the Services. We will use that historic information in accordance with this Policy. By using the Services, you are consenting to our continued use of any such information.
