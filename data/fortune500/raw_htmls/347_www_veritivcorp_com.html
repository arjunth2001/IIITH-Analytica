Data can include certain Personal Data that has been rendered anonymous so that it cannot be used either alone or in combination with other information to identify an individual. We may use data about you in a way that does not identify you from information that you provide us, either separately or together with your Personal Data.

Automatically-Collected Information: We also automatically collect certain usage information from you when you access our Websites. This information includes your mobile device, wireless carrier, the type of browser you are using (e.g., Internet Explorer, Firefox, Safari, Opera, etc.), the third party website from which your visit originated, the operating system you are using (e.g., Vista, Windows XP, Mac OS, Android, iPhone, etc.), the domain name of your Internet service provider (e.g., America Online, NetZero, etc.), the search terms you use on our Websites, the specific web pages you visit, and the duration of your visits.
