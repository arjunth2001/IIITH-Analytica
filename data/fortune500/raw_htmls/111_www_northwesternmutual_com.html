Online Privacy Statement

Effective Date: December 30, 2019
Last Revised: December 30, 2019

This Online Privacy Statement ("Statement") describes how we collect, share and use personal information. This Statement applies to (a) the public-facing website available at northwesternmutual.com and the pages accessible during and after a user ("you" or "your") logs in to a private section of the website (the "Client Website"), and (b) websites owned or operated by the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies. All websites described in the prior sentence are defined collectively for the purposes of this Statement as the "Website".

References to "Northwestern Mutual", the "Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies," "we," "us," "our," or "ours" within this Statement mean The Northwestern Mutual Life Insurance Company, Northwestern Long Term Care Insurance Company, Northwestern Mutual Investment Services, LLC, Northwestern Mutual Wealth Management Company, and the network offices of our Sales Force. Our Sales Force includes the people who sell our products and services, along with their staff.

California residents: If you are a California resident, California law may provide you with additional rights regarding your personal information. To learn more about your California privacy rights, please visit our California Consumer Privacy Act Privacy Policy . Northwestern Mutual does not share your personal information with unaffiliated third parties for third party marketing purposes.

Northwestern Mutual is licensed and registered to provide products and services in the United States only; therefore, this Website and the information contained in it is intended for use in the United States only. By using our Website, you agree that Northwestern Mutual will comply with applicable United States privacy regulations and that Northwestern Mutual can use your personal data in accordance with its Online Privacy Statement and all applicable laws and regulations.

This Website is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years of age. By using the Website, you represent that you are 18 years of age or older. No one under 18 years of age should use the Website.

Collection of Information

We gather information that helps us protect website users, maintain the security of our Website, and for the purposes described below.

The Website gathers two types of information about people who visit Our Website ("Users"):

  1. Voluntary User Information: information and data you provide voluntarily and knowingly, including information and data you submit in or through the Client Website and/or through optional, voluntary contact request forms; and
  2. Automatically Collected Information: tracking information, including (but not limited to) IP address, mobile device, browser type, and home server information.

Northwestern Mutual does not share any of your personal information collected on or through this Website without your permission, except within the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies, as described in this Statement, or as permitted or required by law. This Website does not enable unaffiliated third parties to collect personally identifiable information about your online activities over time and across different websites when you visit this Website. Northwestern Mutual does not sell your information to third parties.

Voluntary User Information

We generally collect information you provide voluntarily and knowingly through two main sections of the Website: (a) the Client Website, accessible only after you log in with your access credentials; and (b) contact request forms. Northwestern Mutual uses information for the purpose you submit it, and shares the information you submit with the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies to service your request, for internal purposes described herein, or as otherwise permitted or required by law. We share any such information with third parties only to the extent necessary to service your request as described herein, or as otherwise permitted or required by law.

Client Website

You can access the Client Website through entering your credentials on a "Log In" page on the Website. Once you have entered the Client Website, you can access certain policy and account information, make limited service requests, and conduct limited transactions. You may also be asked to voluntarily provide information about yourself, and/or your policy or account. In the case of corporate-owned policies, you may be asked to provide information about the corporate owner of the policy or those individuals who are insured under the corporate owned policy.

Some users may be directed to enter product application information on the Client Website, which may include personal and health information ("Online Application Information"). Online Application Information will be used for underwriting purposes and other permissible business purposes. Online Application Information entered by you will be temporarily available on the Client Website for you to continue and complete the application and submission process. We will retain Online Application Information in accordance with applicable law, this Statement, our Customer Privacy Notice , and our internal record retention policies, even if the application is not electronically signed and submitted. We will only consider Online Application Information as part of the underwriting process after it has been incorporated into the electronically signed application form and submitted to us.

Portions of the Client Website are hosted by third parties, which provide certain features and functionality of the Client Website. To use these features or functionality, a third party may request to share your information with Northwestern Mutual. Though each of these third parties is contractually bound to maintain the confidentiality of any information submitted by you and to use such information solely for the purpose of providing the requested services to or on behalf of the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies, each of these third parties govern your information in accordance with its own privacy policy. For more information about the Client Website, please review the Client Website Terms of Use, available when you register or login.

Information you enter into sections of the Client Website may be accessible to the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies. We may also track the pages that you view, including page interactions, and may share this information with the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies to market and offer products, engage in planning, enhance our services, and for other business purposes.

Contact Request Forms

You may submit information through forms on the Website for various purposes. Any information you voluntarily submit is only used by the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies for the purpose of the submission or as specifically described below. The forms described below are a sample of the most-accessed forms on the Website.

  • Contacting a Financial Professional: When you request to be contacted by a Northwestern Mutual Financial Professional, we will ask you to provide basic contact and financial information. We may share this information with the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies to better fulfill your request and for internal marketing, customer relationship management, and servicing purposes.
  • Becoming a Financial Professional: If you are interested in obtaining information about becoming a Northwestern Mutual Financial Professional and indicate that you would like to have a Northwestern Mutual network office recruiter contact you, we will request certain information to facilitate that request. Information you submit in this regard will be routed in an unencrypted format to Northwestern Mutual and will then be shared with the local Northwestern Mutual office that you select during the application process.
  • Corporate Opportunities: If you apply for a position at Northwestern Mutual's corporate offices, you will be asked to register and complete a personal profile to help match your career preferences to available positions. The profile includes (but is not limited to) fields like contact information, career preferences, years of related experience, license/certifications, job type, desired compensation, and location. You can opt-in to receive email notifications of available positions, or you may view and search for available positions without creating a profile. Our corporate opportunities website is hosted by a third party service provider, and as a result, the information collected through the completion of a personal profile is shared with that service provider.
  • Retirement Calculator: When you request an estimate through the retirement calculator functionality, we will ask you to provide basic contact and financial information. We may share this information with the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies to better fulfill your request and for internal marketing, customer relationship management, and servicing purposes.
  • Insurance Calculator: When you request an estimate through the insurance calculator functionality, we will ask you to provide basic contact and financial information. We may share this information with the Northwestern Mutual Family of Companies to better fulfill your request and for internal marketing, customer relationship management, and servicing purposes.
  • Other Forms and Interactive Features: Any other interactive forms or calculators on the Website are for informational purposes only. Northwestern Mutual does not track, retain or use any of the data input into these forms.

Automatically Collected Information

We collect information automatically from our Website through a variety of technologies, including: (a) cookies/web beacons; and (b) third party applications. We also link to third party services and websites, and we are not responsible for the website content or privacy practices of such third parties.

This Website does not contain any functionality to respond to or honor web browser "do not track" signals or other similar mechanisms, and thus does not respond to or honor such signals or mechanisms. Most web browser preferences can be adjusted to alert you that a cookie or tracking code will be sent to your hard drive, or you can refuse to receive a cookie.

Cookies and Web Beacons

The Website uses various storage technologies related to online marketing tools, such as managing our advertising on other Internet websites. In connection with such activities, this Website collects or receives information including (but not limited to) your IP address, device type, date and time of visit, browser type, and activities and the above information is collected or received by or shared with third party vendors who assist us with these activities.

A cookie is a small text file that is sent to a browser when you access the Website. The Website does not require you to use cookies, but it is possible that certain services may not function properly if you choose to refuse to receive a cookie. We use session cookies to ensure better performance and a more enjoyable and consistent user experience from your entry point into the Website. The session cookie is available only during the course of the browser session; the information is not sent to or stored on a computer's hard drive. Closing the browser or logging off will invalidate the session cookie. We also use machine cookies, which do remain on a computer hard drive, to gather aggregated, non-identifiable website statistics through the website analytic services. No personal information is collected using this technology.

We also contract with third-parties to distribute e-mails on our behalf, perform analytics, and to deliver online advertisements on our Website, on other websites and on other applications. Those companies may use third-party cookies, or other devices such as website scripting, pixels, or action tags to collect anonymous information about you when you visit our Website. We use this information to learn which online advertisements attracted you, and to help us analyze our website and the effectiveness of our marketing.

Tracking Technology and Information

To enable us to continually improve on the content we offer you, as well as for systems administration purposes, we capture IP addresses and track your activity on the Website.

To assist us in this process, this Website uses third party website analytic services that use cookies and web beacons to collect various information regarding users' use of this Website, including daily unique visitors, page views, length of time of page views, page navigation paths, time of day of visits, browser type used by users, the Internet Service Provider used to connect to the Internet and this Website, and the type of operating system used by users.

We also use third party services to track information about the performance of our Website. The services collect aggregated data from visits to the Website to help troubleshoot any problems with performance, such as errors in Java Script code or page load times. We use this information to optimize your experience with the Website. The collected data does not contain personally identifiable information and the collected data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Google Maps is a third party service we use to validate and normalize addresses entered by you into the Website through an Application Program Interface, a technology that connects Google to our Website and allows the limited exchange of information. This information is used and retained by us only for the purpose you enter the information, which may include combination with other identifying factors you enter into the Website to fulfill your request to be connected with a Northwestern Mutual Financial Professional in your geographical area. By entering physical address attributes into the Website, you agree to be bound by the Google Maps/Google Earth Additional Terms of Service. We do not track, obtain, or cache your location in any manner except with your prior consent, and you may revoke your consent at any time.

In addition, we partner with other third parties, including third party advertising networks and social media websites, to manage our advertising on other Internet websites. Our third party partners may use cookies, web beacons, JavaScript tags, and other storage technologies to collect or receive information about an IP address, device type, date and time of visit, browser type, and your activities on this website or elsewhere on the Internet and use such information to provide measurement services and targeted advertising for us.

Each third party advertising network maintains their own database and protocols and the use of your information by such third parties is governed by each third party's privacy policy. We do not share individual personal information with these third party advertising providers. Please note that your choice to exercise the right to opt-out with one third party advertising network with which we partner will discontinue your receipt of our ads targeting people like you through that third party partner only, but will not discontinue receipt of our ads targeting people like you through other third party partners and will not terminate the use of cookies, web beacons, JavaScript tags, and other storage technologies by other third party partners to collect or receive information described above. Further, exercising your right to opt-out with a third party advertising partner will not opt you out of being served advertising; generic ads will still be displayed.
