Privacy Policy

AES Corporation is committed to protecting your data and respecting your privacy.  We follow industry best practices, data protection privacy laws, and the new General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) compliance that went into effect on May 25, 2018 to prevent the loss, misuse, and alteration of the information in our possession. We use various security measures to protect the type of information we collect including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. We store information you provide to us on a secure computer server located in a controlled environment that is protected from unauthorized access.

We will never sell our email list or share your private data with any third party for any purpose.  You can manage your Cookie Preferences in your browser’s settings, learn more by visiting the ‘Help’ page of browsers you use.  Your interactions with Social Media features are governed by the Privacy Policy of the companies providing them.

You may update your email preferences or choose to opt out from receiving marketing communications from AES at any time, simply click on the appropriate link in the footer at the bottom of the last email message received from us.  If you wish to submit a request to have all of your contact information permanently deleted, just send us an email to info@aes-corp.com with the word ‘Deletion’ listed in the Subject: line of your message. 

What you get from AES is more than what comes in the box.  Our team is here for your team.
