Privacy Policy
Page Content

This site is owned and operated by Seaboard Foods. We take your privacy seriously and understand the information you provide us while visiting our site is important to you.

This policy may change from time to time so please check back periodically. If there is a material change in the collection, use, or disclosure practices to which you have previously agreed, we will attempt to send you a notice via e-mail if you have provided us with an e-mail address.

Please read the following to learn more about our Privacy Policy.

Information Collection and Use

Seaboard Foods collects personal information when you register for the Merchandising Center or visit certain other pages in our or our affiliates' websites (www.SeaboardFoods.com, www.PrairieFresh.SeaboardFoods.com or www.DailysMeats.SeaboardFoods.com). We may also ask you for information at other times, such as when you report a problem with our site or services or contact us via the corporate e-mail address on the Contact Us page. We use this information to supply you with the services you requested. We will contact you from time to time if you signed up for any of our mailing lists. You can always change your preferences for follow-up contact by logging on and unsubscribing to our services.

We ask for personally identifiable information, such as your name, address, and phone number to register for certain parts of our site. However, we do not require you to register to use the public areas of the site. This additional registration step allows you to use more functions, such as the Sales Center, within the sites.

Seaboard Foods automatically receives and records information on our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, cookie information and the page you request. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. We use cookies to allow faster and easier access to your account information, monitor traffic on the site and measure traffic patterns to improve functionality.

We use the information you voluntarily provide and the information we collect automatically to provide you with customized content, fulfill your requests for products and services, contact you, conduct research, monitor traffic on the site and provide anonymous reporting for internal and external uses. We may also share your information with our corporate affiliates.

We also may do research on our users' demographics and behavior based on the information provided to us upon registration and gathered from transaction activity, server log files and surveys. This research may be compiled, analyzed or sold on an aggregated basis, which does not include information identifying individual users.

We also may disclose account information in special cases when we have reason to believe that disclosing this information is necessary to identify, contact or bring legal action against someone who may be violating any terms and conditions of use posed on our website, or may otherwise be causing injury to or interference with (either intentionally or unintentionally) our rights or property, other website users or customers, or anyone else that could be harmed by such activities. We may disclose or access your information when we believe in good faith that the law requires it and for administrative and other purposes that we deem necessary to maintain, service and improve our products and services.

As we continue to develop our business, we may buy or sell businesses or assets. In such transactions, confidential user information generally is one of the transferred business assets. In the event of a transaction involving the sale of some or all of our businesses, customer and site visitor information may be one of the transferred assets.

We will only provide your contact information to our unaffiliated business partners, such as supermarkets or restaurants, if you have given us permission when you registered with the site.

In the course of doing business, we may employ others to perform functions on our behalf, including packaging and mailing, answering customer questions about products or services, sending postal mail, and processing data. These groups may have access to personally identifiable information; however they are prohibited from using it for other purposes and they are required to maintain all information in confidence.

Seaboard Foods offers its customers choices for the collection, use and sharing of personal information. You may contact us at info@seaboardfoods.com if you wish to view, edit or delete your personal information from our database, and we will use commercially reasonable efforts to accommodate your request.

While Seaboard Foods encourages parents and guardians to explore our site with their children, we recognize the importance of protecting privacy where children are involved and do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information online from children under the age of 13. If a child under the age of 13 has provided us with personally identifiable information online, we ask that a parent or guardian contact us. We do not provide knowingly any personal information, regardless of its source, to any third party from anyone under 13 years of age and do not use knowingly any personal information collected from anyone under 13 years of age for any marketing or promotional purposes.

We do provide links to other sites from our Website. However, we cannot guarantee the content of the sites we have linked. All websites have different privacy policies and you should check the privacy policy of the specific website you are visiting to understand your rights. The information practices of other websites linked to our websites are not covered by this Privacy Policy.
