We use this information to help us improve our Website and to deliver a better and more personalized service by enabling us to:

  • Store information about your preferences, allowing us to customize our Website according to your individual interests.
  • Speed up your searches.
  • Recognize you when you return to our Website.
The technologies we use for this automatic data collection may include:
  • Cookies (or browser cookies). A cookie is a small file placed on the hard drive of your computer. You may refuse to accept browser cookies by modifying the appropriate settings on your browser or other software. However, if you select this setting you may be unable to access certain parts of our Website. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so that it will refuse cookies, our system will issue cookies when you direct your browser to our Website.

The Website uses Google Analytics to collect non-personally identifiable data that we use to monitor traffic on the Website in order to improve the user experience of the Website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect data regarding your use of the Website, including navigation through the pages, what content was viewed, how you arrived to specific content, the time spent viewing specific content, your ISP provider and geographic information relating thereto, and other non-personally identifiable data. The information generated by the cookie about your use of the Website is transmitted to Google for compiling statistical reports regarding the foregoing. Please review the Google Analytics privacy policy for additional information.
