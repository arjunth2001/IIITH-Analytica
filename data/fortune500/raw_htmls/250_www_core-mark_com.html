Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Core-Mark International (“Core-Mark”) Web Site (the “Site”). This Site is intended to be an information resource for Web site visitors and our clients. This privacy policy is intended to inform visitors of the ways in which this Site collects information and how we use it.

Information We Collect
We collect information which visitors provide. For example, users may provide us their e-mail addresses to request information, or send us their mailing addresses so we may send materials or information that visitors have requested. In this way, we may collect the following information about our visitors: name, title, business address and telephone number, product usage and attitudes, as well as preferences for content, numbers of visits and length of time spent on the Site. Visitors whose businesses are Core-Mark retail store clients can register for our eBusiness applications. Registered users may be asked to provide additional information about their businesses.

We use the information our visitors provide to respond to questions or requests regarding the Core-Mark group of companies (the “Core-Mark Companies”), our products and services, or our Site; to improve Site content; and to send visitors periodic mailings and/or e-mails or call visitors about Core-Mark services and products or upcoming events.

Cookies
Cookies are pieces of information stored by a visitor’s web browser on behalf of a Web site and returned to the Web site on request. Our Site uses cookies when a registered user is logged into our Site to help us manage the Site, improve features of the Site and understand usage patterns. The information that may be maintained by a cookie may include: the pages visited on the Site; the date and time of the visit; the internet address (URL or IP address) of the referring site; the domain name and IP address from which the access occurred; the version of browser used; the capabilities of the browser; and the search terms used on our search engines. The Site may use cookies for two purposes: to carry data about your current session at the Site from one Web page to the next, and to identify you to the Site. A cookie cannot read data off a visitor’s hard drive.

Some pages of our Site may not function properly if the cookies are turned off. Visitors should consult their web browser’s Help documentation for more information about how to turn cookies on and off for their browser.

In the course of ensuring network security and consistent service for all users, Core-Mark employs software programs to do such things as monitor network traffic, identify unauthorized access or access to nonpublic information, detect computer viruses and other software that might damage Core-Mark computer systems or the network, and monitor and tune the performance of the Core-Mark network. In the course of such monitoring, these programs may detect such information as e-mail headers, addresses from network packets, and other information. Information from these activities is used only for the purpose of maintaining the security and performance of Core-Mark’s networks and computer systems.

Use of Personal Information
The personal information we maintain will be held by Core-Mark and may be used by Core-Mark, other companies within the Core-Mark group (details of which are available on request), or Core-Mark’s agents, partners or licensees. We do not sell, exchange or otherwise distribute visitor information without visitors’ consent, except to the extent required by law, in accordance with visitors’ instructions, or as set out below

Agents and Service Providers.
* We sometimes contract with other companies and individuals to perform functions or services on our behalf, such as hosting this Site, sending e-mail messages, and making phone calls on our behalf. They may have access to personal information, such as addresses, needed to perform their functions, but are restricted from using it for purposes other than providing services for Core-Mark.

Business Transfers.
* As we continue to develop our business, we might sell or buy assets. In such transactions, Web site visitor information generally is one of the transferred business assets. Therefore, if either Core-Mark itself or substantially all of Core-Mark assets were acquired, visitor information may be one of the transferred assets.

Legal Matters.
* Core-Mark reserves the right to disclose any information about visitors and their use of this Site without prior permission if Core-Mark has a good faith belief that such action is necessary to: (a) protect and defend the rights, property or safety of Core-Mark or its affiliates or subsidiaries, other users of this Site, or the public; (b) enforce the Terms of Use for this Site; or (c) respond to claims that any content violates the rights of third-parties. We may also disclose information as we deem necessary to satisfy any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

External Links
This Web site may include links to other Web sites whose privacy policies we do not control. Once visitors leave our servers, use of any information visitors provide is governed by the privacy policy of the operator of the third party site.

Information Security
All information gathered on our Site is securely stored within the Core-Mark-controlled database. The database is stored on servers secured behind a firewall and access to the servers is password-protected and is strictly limited. However, as effective as our security measures are, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our database, nor can we guarantee that visitor information will not be intercepted while being transmitted to us over the Internet. We ask that visitors do their part, by maintaining strictly confidential any computer passwords and eBusiness usernames and passwords used to access the Internet or this Site.

Storage and Access to Information
This Site and all information collected through this Site is collected, stored, and processed in the United States and Canada.

You can ask to review the information that we maintain about you or to correct, update, or delete any inaccurate information that we maintain about you at any time. Your request must be submitted in writing by e-mail at privacy@core-mark.com or by mail at the address below. We may take a reasonable period of time to respond. If you request the deletion or change of inaccurate information on our system, such information may be retained for a period in our backup systems as a precaution against systems failures. Some information may be retained for longer periods as required by law, contract or auditing requirements.

Your Information and Opting-Out
If you do not wish to receive telephone contact or periodic mailings or emails concerning new products and services or upcoming events, you may opt-out of receiving such information by sending us a written request to the address listed below or by following the opt-out instructions in an e-mail you receive from us.
