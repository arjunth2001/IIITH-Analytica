Privacy Statement

Our Privacy Commitment

Calpine Corporation (“Calpine”) cares about our customers and is committed to protecting their privacy. This Privacy Statement (referred to herein as “Privacy Statement” or “Statement”) is intended to inform you of the type of personal information Calpine or its subsidiaries collect from you, how it is used and what steps are taken to ensure that your personal information is kept confidential and safe.

Calpine collects no personal information from visitors to this website. Calpine’s retail subsidiaries (Champion Energy Services, Calpine Energy Solutions, and North American Power & Gas) collect only personal information that is provided voluntarily by its customers. Such personal information collected by these subsidiaries is primarily used to provide retail electricity services. The subsidiaries’ websites are linked from Calpine’s website, and you may read their privacy policies on those sites. 

Complaints

Calpine is committed to working with consumers to obtain a fair and rapid resolution of any complaints or disputes about privacy. Please send us your questions or comments regarding our privacy practices by either sending us an email at CalpineWeb@calpine.com or writing a letter to:

Links to Other Sites

Calpine’s website may contain links to other sites. Calpine is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of such websites. We recommend you carefully read the privacy statements or policies of each site you visit.

Statement Changes

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Statement at any time, so please check back periodically so you are familiar with our current Statement. If in the future we wish to use your information in a manner not previously disclosed, we will post changes to the Statement here and if significant, we will provide a more prominent notice of these changes. Continued use of our websites after receiving notice of a change in our Statement indicates your consent to the use of newly submitted information in accordance with the amended Calpine Privacy Statement.

If you have questions about these Terms and Conditions, do not hesitate to contact us at corporatecommunications@calpine.com.

Acceptance of Our Privacy Practices

Acceptance of this Privacy Statement is signaled by using the company’s website. By accepting this Statement, the user agrees to adhere to the terms and conditions posted on the website and contained herein. Visitors, callers, customers or potential customers that do not agree with any of these terms should not use this website.
The English version of this Privacy Statement shall always prevail in case of conflicts between it and any translations of our Privacy Statement.
