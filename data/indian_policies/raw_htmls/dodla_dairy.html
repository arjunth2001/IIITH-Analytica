Privacy Policy

Home Privacy Policy

The official website of Dodla Dairy Limited is www.dodladairy.com. The company gives utmost priority for information privacy and respects the privacy of every visitor to it’s website. Dodla Dairy does not capture any personal information of the visitors automatically. The visitors may be asked to provide certain information like name, phone number and email id under Business Enquiry or Feedback pages, only to give a response or provide more details about Dodla Dairy products and services.

Dodla Dairy Limited assures that the information gathered would be used only for internal purposes and will not be sold or provided to third parties. Visitor personally identifiable information will be adequately protected from loss, misuse, unauthorised access or disclosure, alteration, or destruction and it would only be used to respond in timely manner and to fulfil the request of the visitor.

However, the company retains the right to contact the person who has submitted the information by any means (emails, phone etc) at any time unless otherwise specified. Visitor privacy is most respected and in case, anyone does not want to receive any communication from Dodla Dairy, all steps would be taken not to contact such person / business entity in future and details would be removed from the contact list.

Use of Cookies

A cookie is a piece of software code that an internet web site sends to your browser when you access information at that site. A cookie is stored as a simple text file on your computer or mobile device by a website’s server and only that server will be able to retrieve or read the contents of that cookie. Cookies let you navigate between pages efficiently as they store your preferences, and generally improve your experience of a website

Dodla Dairy uses only non-persistent cookies a.k.a per-session cookies which help seamless navigation through this website. These cookies do not collect personal information on users and they are deleted as soon as you leave the website.
