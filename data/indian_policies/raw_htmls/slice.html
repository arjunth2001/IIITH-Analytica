    Slice uses the information collected from You or about You for providing Service to You. When, You set up Your account with Slice, as part of the lending and repayment process, We collect personal information You give such as first and last name; home address, billing address, or other physical address; email addresses; telephone numbers; identity information like PAN number, Address proof, date of birth, gender, contacts, 3 months' bank statement or rental agreement etc (“Account Opening Information”)… In addition, We also collect and store certain information including Your usernames, passwords, and other security-related information that You use for Our Website/Portal in relation to Our Services. ("Account Information"). In general, Slice will use the information collected from You for the purposes for which You provided such information and as set out below.


    Slice collects the following information (“Personal Information”):

  • Account Opening Information and Account Information
  • self-declared information like name, email, educational details (like college, course name, year of graduation etc.), employment details (company name, salary, designation, employment history, PF Preference etc.), address details, details of parents, reference contact (name and number), photo, Date of Birth, nationality etc. and public information on Your social accounts (Facebook and LinkedIn).
  • Documents: Soft copy of documents including Bank Statements, Rental Agreement, Address Proof, Identity Proof, Salary Slip and Form 16.
  • SMS : We periodically collect Your SMS’s to understand Your financial and risk profile by analysing transactional SMSs and SMSs received by You from financial institutions. This helps Us in providing You with quicker approvals and higher credit limits. You will not be able to use the Portal if you disable this access
  • Camera: We require the camera information permission to provide you an easy/smooth experience and to enable you to click photos of your KYC documents along with other requisite documents and upload the same on the Portal during your loan application journey.
  • Location: We collect and monitor Your location to verify Your current address, ensures serviceability, hassle-free search of company/college name and prevent any fraudulent activity. We collect the location data from you in two ways: (i) when you add the pin code as part of your Account Opening Information; and (ii) from your mobile device when enabled by you to do so. We collect this data when our Portal is running in the background of your mobile device. You will not be able to use the Portal if you disable the location services
  • Contact: Our Portal requires this permission to detect references and to auto fill the contact details during credit profile application for seamless Customer journey. We also collect and monitor Your contacts’ information which includes name, phone numbers, account type, contact last modified, favourites and other optional data like relationship and address to enrich Your credit profile. This also helps Us to assess Your risk and network profile and provide You regular credit limit enhancements. You will not be able to use the Portal if you disable this access.
  • Installed Apps: We collect a list of the installed applications’ metadata information which includes the application name, package name, installed time, updated time, version name and version code of each installed application on your device to assess your creditworthiness and enrich your profile with pre-approved customized loan offers.. This also includes having access and permission to send you messages and notifications via your social media and instant messaging applications. You will not be able to use the Portal if you disable this access.
  • Transaction information: We collect transaction information related to the use of our Services, including the type of Services requested, date and time the Service was provided, loan availed, interest payable, EMI selected, and payment method. Additionally, if someone uses your promotion code, we may associate your name with that person and their usage of the Portal.
  • Device Data- When You browse Our Website/Portal or apps We also automatically receive Your computer’s internet protocol (IP) address in order to provide Us with information that helps Us learn about Your browser and operating system, software, the date and time of visiting the Portals, the type of device being used (including the hardware models) the browser being used etc.
  • Storage: This permission is required so that users' documents can be securely downloaded and saved on users' phones and upload the right documents for a faster approval and disbursal of the loan. This helps provide a very smooth and seamless experience while using the Portal.
  • Feedback Data and Other Data: This includes the following:
    • If You call our call center/office, we may record information provided by you to service you or record the calls for quality and training purposes;
    • Data you input when you participate in our referral programs or use any discount codes offered by us;
    • If you provide any feedback or comments to us on the Portal.
  • Third Party Information- We may also be working closely with third parties (including, for example, credit information bureaus, business partners, technical sub-contractors, analytics providers, search information providers) and may lawfully receive information about you from such sources. Such data may be shared internally and combined with data collected on the Portal.
  • such other information as provided in this Policy
